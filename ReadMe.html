Welcome to the Konnect Board, a Turn-Based Game.

    How to Play

  1. If you Want, Do Some Configurations, (or Just Go with the Default Settings),
    • Enter your Own Custom Total Rows and Columns of the Board. The Default is 4x4.
      The Custom Range of Row-Column can be upto 12x12.
    • Enter your Own Custom Konnect-Goal of the Game. The Default is 3.
      This Defines the Difficulty of the Game (To Occupy How Many Consecutive Squares).
      The Custom Range of Konnect-Goal can be upto 8.
    • Enter the Total Nummber of Players who wants to Play the Game. By Default, Its 2-Player.
      The Custom Range of Players can be upto 8.
  2. When You are Done Configuring, Click the Play Button. Lets the Fun Begin,
    After that, You can Click the New Game Button at any Time, if you didn't like the Configuartions (or Whatever).
  3. On Startup, Its Player 1's Turn.
    On you Turn, CLick on any Square on the Given Board to Mark on that Place.
  4. Occupy Consecutive Places eiher Vertically, Horizontally, or Diagonally.
    The First Player to Occupy those Konnect-Number of Squares (that you Set) would Won the Game.
  5. The Game would result in a Draw, if the whole Board fills out, and the Players were still unable to Complete the Goal.
  6. After Game Completion, You can Play it Again!, by Clicking on the New Game Button.

    Hints

  • Customize the Board-Size:3x3, and Konnect-Goal:3, to make it the Classical Tic-Tac-Toe Game.
  • Customize the Board-Size:7x7, and Konnect-Goal:4, to make it the Classical Connect-4 Game.

    Note

  • The Override is not Allowed.
  • You Can Mute/Play the Background Music by Clicking on the Speaker Icon on the Bottom Left.
  • If you want to Customize the Player-Marks, You Would get that Feature Soon.
    You can then Enter your own Custom Player Marks, by entering whatever Symbol you like from the Keyboard.
